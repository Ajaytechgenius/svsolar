Advertising — Google AdSense

This website uses Google AdSense, a third-party advertising service provided by Google LLC, to display advertisements. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies (including the DoubleClick DART cookie) to serve ads based on your visits to this site and other websites on the internet.

What Google Collects

When you visit pages on this site, Google and its partners may use cookies and similar technologies to collect and process data such as your IP address, device and browser information, and browsing behaviour, in order to:

  • Show ads that may be relevant to your interests
  • Measure ad performance and prevent fraudulent activity
  • Limit how many times you see the same ad

SVSOLAR does not control the content of ads served by Google, nor does it have access to the cookies Google places on your device. This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with Google's Advertising Policies and Google's Privacy Policy.

Your Choices

You can control the use of cookies for personalised advertising by visiting:

  • Google Ads Settings — to opt out of personalised ads from Google
  • aboutads.info — to opt out of interest-based ads from participating third-party vendors

You can also disable cookies entirely through your browser settings, though this may affect how some parts of this site function.

Data Processing & Applicable Law (India)

As this website is operated for users in India, personal data collected in connection with advertising on this site is handled in accordance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act) and applicable rules issued under the Information Technology Act, 2000. SVSOLAR does not sell or share personal data with third parties for purposes other than the display of advertising through Google AdSense as described above.

Children's Privacy

This site and its advertising are not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children, and Google's advertising policies restrict serving personalised ads to users known to be minors.
